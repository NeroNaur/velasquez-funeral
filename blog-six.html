Making Funeral Services More Affordable: A Guide to Installment Payments

Losing a loved one is emotionally draining enough without the added stress of immediate financial burden. Understanding funeral costs and available payment options can help your family make dignified choices while managing expenses responsibly. Learn how Velasquez Funeral & Chapel's flexible payment plans make professional funeral services accessible to all Cabiao families.

Published by Velasquez Funeral & Chapel

Introduction: Dignity Shouldn't Depend on Immediate Payment

When a loved one passes away, families are already coping with grief, making arrangements, and coordinating with relatives. The last thing anyone should worry about during this difficult time is how to immediately pay for funeral services. Yet the reality is that funeral costs can be significant, and many families simply don't have thousands of pesos readily available.

At Velasquez Funeral & Chapel, we've served Cabiao, Nueva Ecija families for over 50 years, and we understand this challenge intimately. That's why we created our flexible funeral packages with installment payment options – because every family deserves to give their loved one a dignified farewell, regardless of their immediate financial situation.

This guide will help you understand what goes into funeral costs, explain our payment options, and show you how to plan for funeral expenses without sacrificing the quality of care your loved one deserves.

Important: Professional funeral care should be accessible to everyone. Our 30% down payment plan and flexible installments ensure no family has to choose between dignity and affordability.

Understanding Funeral Costs: What You're Actually Paying For

Understanding what's included in funeral packages helps you make informed decisions

Many people are surprised by funeral costs because they don't realize everything that goes into providing professional funeral services. Let's break down the major components of a typical funeral package to help you understand where your money goes.

1. Professional Services and Staff

The expertise of a funeral director and support staff is fundamental to any funeral service. This includes:

  • 24/7 Availability – We're available any time, day or night, to collect your loved one and begin arrangements
  • Funeral planning consultation – Experienced guidance through all decisions and options
  • Coordination services – Managing logistics with cemeteries, churches, government offices, and vendors
  • Documentation assistance – Help with death certificates, permits, and paperwork
  • On-site support – Staff present during wake and funeral to assist with any needs

2. Embalming and Preparation

Professional embalming services are essential for multi-day wakes in the Philippines' tropical climate:

  • Embalming procedure – Professional preservation to maintain your loved one's appearance
  • Cosmetic preparation – Skilled restoration to provide a peaceful, natural appearance
  • Dressing and grooming – Respectful preparation with the clothing you provide
  • Ongoing care – Maintenance throughout the wake period

3. Casket Selection

The casket is typically one of the largest expenses in a funeral package:

  • Materials – Ranges from affordable wood and metal to premium hardwoods
  • Interior lining – Comfortable, dignified interior fabrics
  • Hardware and design – Handles, locks, and decorative elements
  • Size and style – Various options to match preferences and budget

Our funeral packages include appropriate caskets for each price level, ensuring quality regardless of which package you choose.

4. Chapel or Venue Services

If you choose our chapel venue for wake services:

  • Facility use – Access to our climate-controlled chapel
  • Seating and setup – Chairs, tables, and arrangement of the viewing area
  • Sound and lighting – Professional equipment for music and atmosphere
  • Utilities and maintenance – Air conditioning, cleaning, and facility upkeep
  • Security – Professional security during wake hours

5. Transportation

Moving your loved one safely and respectfully requires specialized vehicles:

  • Initial transfer – From place of death to our facility
  • Funeral procession – Hearse for transport to church and cemetery
  • Staff vehicles – For our team to provide support throughout services

6. Burial Coordination and Permits

Burial arrangements involve multiple requirements:

  • Cemetery coordination – Scheduling and logistics with the burial site
  • Government permits – Burial permits and required documentation
  • Grave opening/closing – If not included in cemetery fees
  • Burial vault or liner – If required by the cemetery

7. Additional Items and Services

Depending on your chosen package, you may also receive:

  • Flowers and wreaths – Floral arrangements for the casket and venue
  • Memorial materials – Programs, guest books, acknowledgment cards
  • Candles and religious items – For prayers and services
  • Viewing accessories – Kneelers, stands, draping

Transparency matters: At Velasquez Funeral & Chapel, we provide itemized price breakdowns so you know exactly what's included in each package. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Flexible Payment Options: Making Dignity Affordable

Our team works with families to create payment plans that fit their financial situation

At Velasquez Funeral & Chapel, we believe that financial constraints should never force a family to choose between a dignified service and their budget. That's why we've developed the most flexible funeral payment plans in Cabiao, Nueva Ecija.

The Velasquez 30% Down Payment Plan

Our signature payment option makes affordable funeral services accessible to families who need time to gather funds:

  • Only 30% down – You pay just 30% of the total package cost to begin services immediately
  • Immediate service – We begin caring for your loved one right away, without waiting for full payment
  • Flexible installment terms – The remaining 70% can be paid in installments over a period we agree upon together
  • No interest charges – We don't add interest to installment payments (terms may vary based on payment period)
  • Customized schedule – Work with our funeral director to create a payment schedule that fits your family's financial situation
  • Multiple payment methods – Cash, bank transfer, or other agreed-upon payment methods

How the 30% Plan Works: A Real Example

Let's say you choose a funeral package that costs ₱60,000 total:

  • Down payment: ₱18,000 (30% of ₱60,000)
  • Remaining balance: ₱42,000 (70% of ₱60,000)
  • Monthly payments: If paid over 6 months = ₱7,000/month
  • Or over 12 months: ₱3,500/month

This flexibility means families don't need to have the full ₱60,000 available immediately. With just ₱18,000, you can provide your loved one with a complete, dignified funeral service, then pay the balance over time as your finances allow.

Alternative Payment Arrangements

We understand that every family's situation is unique. If the 30% plan doesn't fit your circumstances, we're willing to discuss:

  • Higher down payments – Reduce your installment amounts by paying more upfront
  • Extended payment periods – Longer terms for lower monthly payments
  • Family contribution plans – Multiple family members contributing portions
  • Special circumstances – We consider cases of extreme financial hardship with compassion

What Happens If You Can't Make a Payment?

Life is unpredictable, and we understand that sometimes circumstances change. If you're struggling to make a payment:

  • Communicate with us – Contact us immediately; we'll work with you
  • Flexible rescheduling – We can often adjust your payment schedule
  • Compassionate approach – We've served this community for 50+ years; we treat you like family
  • No aggressive collection – We believe in working together, not creating more stress

Our Promise: We've never refused service to a family because of their financial situation. If you're willing to work with us, we'll find a solution that provides dignity for your loved one while respecting your budget.

Planning Ahead vs. Emergency Situations

Pre-planning funeral arrangements can significantly reduce financial stress and emotional burden

While our flexible payment plans help during unexpected loss, planning ahead provides even greater peace of mind and financial benefits.

The Benefits of Pre-Planning

When you arrange and pay for funeral services in advance:

  • Lock in today's prices – Protect against future cost increases
  • More time to save – Pay gradually while you're still healthy and working
  • Remove burden from family – Your loved ones won't have to make difficult decisions while grieving
  • Ensure your wishes are followed – Specify exactly how you want your funeral conducted
  • Better planning options – More time means better package choices and customization
  • Potential savings – Pre-need plans often include incentives or discounts

Emergency Payment Options

When death is unexpected and you haven't planned ahead, our payment plans become essential:

  • Immediate service – No delays while you arrange finances
  • Dignified options – Choose based on what's right, not just what you can pay today
  • Time to gather funds – Family members can contribute over time
  • Reduced immediate stress – Focus on grieving and remembering, not scrambling for money

Starting Your Pre-Planning

It's never too early to consider pre-planning. Even young families can benefit from having a plan in place. Our funeral planning consultations help you:

  • Understand your options and costs
  • Choose the right funeral package for your needs
  • Set up a comfortable payment schedule
  • Document your wishes for your family
  • Gain peace of mind knowing everything is arranged

Did you know? Pre-planning doesn't mean paying everything immediately. You can start with small monthly payments and have years to complete your plan while locking in current prices.

One common concern we hear is families worrying that choosing a more affordable funeral service means settling for poor quality or disrespectful treatment. This is absolutely not true, especially at Velasquez Funeral & Chapel.

Quality in Every Package

Whether you choose our basic package or our premium option, you receive:

  • The same professional care – Every family receives our full attention and expertise
  • Respectful treatment – Your loved one is cared for with equal dignity regardless of package price
  • Experienced staff – Our team has decades of experience serving Cabiao families

Understanding Package Differences

Higher-priced packages typically offer:

  • Premium casket materials – Hardwood, metal, or luxury finishes vs. quality standard options
  • Additional amenities – More flowers, upgraded memorial materials, special vehicles
  • Extended services – Longer wake periods or additional locations
  • Upgraded venues – Larger chapel spaces for very big gatherings

But the fundamentals – professional embalming, respectful care, qualified staff, and complete burial arrangements – are included in every package.

Making Informed Choices

Our funeral director will help you:

  • Understand what's essential vs. what's optional
  • Choose a package that honors your loved one within your budget
  • Add specific items that matter most to your family
  • Remove elements you don't need or want
  • Create a customized plan that's both meaningful and affordable
