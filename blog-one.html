Immediate Steps After a Loved One Passes

A calm, organized guide for families during the first 24 hours of loss.

Introduction

Losing a loved one is a deeply emotional experience. During this time, it is vital to take calm, organized steps while continuing to support each other emotionally.

1. Confirm the Death

  • Call medical staff immediately.
  • Coordinate to obtain the official death certificate.

2. Notify Important Contacts

  • Reach out to family members and close friends.
  • Inform employers or relevant organizations if needed.

3. Care for the Deceased

  • Arrange professional transfer to our funeral home facility.
  • Consult with our staff regarding preservation or embalming services.

4. Plan Funeral or Memorial

  • Choose a funeral home to guide your arrangements.
  • Schedule the service, visitation, and burial or cremation details.

Conclusion: Take it one step at a time and lean on family and community support. We are available 24/7 in Cabiao to assist you through every stage.
