Common Questions

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions. If you don't see your question here, please don't hesitate to contact us.

What should I do first when a loved one passes?

First, if the death occurred at a hospital or care facility, notify the staff. If at home, contact emergency services or hospice if applicable. Then call us at your earliest convenience - we're available 24/7 to guide you through the next steps and arrange for the transfer of your loved one to our facility.

How soon should I contact you?

You can contact us immediately or whenever you feel ready. There's no rush - take the time you need. We're here when you're ready to begin making arrangements, whether that's right away or a few days later.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment arrangements starting with a 30% down payment. We understand that financial considerations are important, and we'll work with you to create a payment plan that fits your family's budget.

Can we meet with you before making any decisions?

Absolutely. We encourage families to visit us for a no-pressure consultation. We'll explain all your options, answer your questions, and help you understand what to expect. There's no obligation to proceed after the consultation.

What if I live outside Cabiao?

We serve families throughout Nueva Ecija and surrounding areas. We can arrange phone consultations and coordinate services even if you're unable to visit us in person initially.
